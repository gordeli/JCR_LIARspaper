The current study relates to exercise apps designed to stretch a stiff neck during a long day at the desk.

The task on the next page is timed. You will need to complete the task within 90 seconds. 

When ready to begin the task, press the arrow button.

Scientists found that a stiff neck can influence your thoughts. When you do not move your neck for a long time, your thoughts become less focused, it is harder to come up with good ideas, and you are less efficient.
Consider a new app designed to help people stretch their necks during a long working day at the desk. The app features several exercises and some information.
A preliminary version of the app contains several screens with exercises.
